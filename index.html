TEA RECIPE



INGREDINTS:

  • 1 Cup Milk.

  • 1/4 Cup Water.

  • 1 TableSpoon Sugar(As You Required).

  • 1 TableSpoon Tea Powder.



METHOD:

  1. Add Tea Powder And Sugar In Non-Stick Sause Pan.

  2. Add Water In Non-Stick Sause Pan Then Boil For 2 Minutes In Low Flame.

  3. After Boiling 2 Minutes.Add Milk.

  4. Boil It For 5 Minutes In Medium Flame.

  5. After Boiling.Put Tea In A Cup.

  6. Then Enjoy The Tea.
